Vulnerabilities will be rated according to the following severity levels\+:


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item {\bfseries{Critical}}\+: Immediate threat to confidentiality, integrity, or availability of sensitive data or systems
\item {\bfseries{High}}\+: Significant risk to confidentiality, integrity, or availability
\item {\bfseries{Medium}}\+: Moderate risk to confidentiality, integrity, or availability
\item {\bfseries{Low}}\+: Minimal risk to confidentiality, integrity, or availability
\item {\bfseries{Informational}}\+: No direct security impact, but could be used in combination with other vulnerabilities
