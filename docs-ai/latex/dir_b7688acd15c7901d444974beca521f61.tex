ECMAScript spec abstract operations.

Every operation is available by edition/year and by name -\/ for example, {\ttfamily es-\/abstract/2020/\+Call} gives you the {\ttfamily Call} operation from ES2020, {\ttfamily es-\/abstract/5/\+Type} gives you the {\ttfamily Type} operation from ES5.

All abstract operations are also available under an {\ttfamily es5}/{\ttfamily es2015}/{\ttfamily es2016}/{\ttfamily es2017}/{\ttfamily es2018}/{\ttfamily es2019}/{\ttfamily es2020}/{\ttfamily es2021} entry point, and as a property on the {\ttfamily main} export, but using deep imports is highly encouraged for bundle size and performance reasons.
