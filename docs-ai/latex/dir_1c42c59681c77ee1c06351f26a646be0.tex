 Show a tree of the processes inside your system inside your terminal.


\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{npx\ pidtree\ \$PPID}

\end{DoxyCode}
 Just replace {\ttfamily \$PPID} with one of the pids inside your system.

Or don\textquotesingle{}t pass anything if you want all the pids inside your system.


\begin{DoxyCode}{0}

\end{DoxyCode}
