\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{quote}
Determine the \href{https://unicode.org/reports/tr11/}{\texttt{ East Asian Width}} of a Unicode character \end{quote}


\begin{quote}
East Asian Width categorizes Unicode characters based on their occupied space in East Asian typography, which helps in text layout and alignment, particularly in languages like Japanese, Chinese, and Korean. \end{quote}
